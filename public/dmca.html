What is DMCA?

The Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) provides a process for copyright holders to request removal of infringing content. 4reels does not host any media files — we embed streams from third-party sources. However, we will forward valid DMCA notices to the appropriate hosting providers and remove embeds upon receipt of a valid notice.

Before You Submit

  • Ensure you are the copyright holder or authorised to act on their behalf.
  • Provide the specific URL(s) where the infringing content appears.
  • False DMCA notices may result in legal liability under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f).
  • We will respond to all valid notices within 72 hours.
