Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. This policy explains how Luvenn collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Luvenn is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require account registration and collect minimal data:

  • Usage data: Anonymous page views, search queries, and general browsing patterns used solely to improve the service.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and general location (country level) via server logs. These logs are automatically purged after 30 days.
  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you submit a DMCA or contact form.

2. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. Your preferences (such as selected player server) may be stored in your browser's local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

Luvenn embeds video content from third-party streaming providers. These providers have their own privacy policies and may collect data when you interact with their embedded players. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of:

  • VidEasy (player.videasy.net)
  • ZFlix (zflix.cc)
  • VidSrc (vidsrc.me)
  • The Movie Database / TMDB (themoviedb.org)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data for analytical purposes. DMCA notices and contact form submissions are stored securely and used only to respond to your request.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we hold. All connections to Luvenn are encrypted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us via the contact form. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Luvenn is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Luvenn after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use our contact form.
